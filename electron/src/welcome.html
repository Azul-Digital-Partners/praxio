Welcome to Praxio

A local-first workspace for running and coordinating AI agents on your Mac.

What Praxio is

Praxio runs an embedded server, database, and a UI for managing agents, tasks, and projects. Everything runs on this Mac — no account required to get started.

What stays on your Mac

Your projects, tasks, agent settings, and database all live in ~/Library/Application Support/Praxio. Praxio doesn't upload your data anywhere. When an agent calls an external model or tool, that call goes directly from your Mac to the provider you configured.

You can revisit this from the Praxio menu.
